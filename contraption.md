<h2> <em>CONCEPT</em></h2>
	
  <p>Contraption is a  sound sculpture comprising five multichannel towers, each containing several rings of eight speakers. The speakers are hidden behind perforated steel covers, such that the audience is confronted with five featureless industrial-looking pillars. As with all <a href="grids.html">GRIDs</a> installations, the proximity of the speakers accommodates high  spatial detail in the sonic outcome.  </p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><a target="_blank" href="images/Contraption/contraptionDiagram.jpg"><img src="images/Contraption/contraptionDiagram.jpg" alt="Contraption Concept" width="800" height="339" border="0"/></a></p>
<p>Each ring of speakers acts as a 'cog' in an imagined machine. Visitors to the installation are empowered to trigger rotary gestures which  travel up and down the pillar or  catalyse similar events on other pillars. Longterm gestures across the installation settle into stable acousmatic scenes.</p>
