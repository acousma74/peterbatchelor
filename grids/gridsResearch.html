OVERVIEW • QUESTIONS • CONTEXT • DISSEMINATION

 

RESEARCH QUESTIONS

How can dense geometric multichannel configurations be used to make high-quality, detailed spatial sound environments?

How can such extreme multichannel arrangements be developed and managed in an affordable way?

What new acousmatic compositional possibilities are afforded by such multichannel arrangements in conjunction with non-standard presentation strategies?
     - spherical arrangement; individualised experience (Beyond)
     - flat panel ceiling arrangement; ambulatory exploration (Cascade)
     - rotational speaker movement; ambulatory exploration (Orbits)
     - multiple cluster speaker arrangements; ambulatory/interactive exploration (Contraption)

How can a listening experience be made more intimate through the exploration of multichannel environments?

How can an acousmatic experience be made to encourage closer listening to the wider environment?

How can acousmatic composition be made to reach a wider audience?
