The last few weeks have been a frenzy of final preparations for *Orbits*, a collaborative installation by Pete Batchelor and Mike Blow in the Clore Ballroom at the Southbank Centre. 

The piece was supported by the Philharmonia Orchestra and used recordings of some of the instrumentalists alongside NASA space sounds.
