# installations
*a series of massively multichannel installation works*

---

## Chamberlain Clocktower Sonic Intervention
[![Chamberlain Clocktower Sonic Intervention]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/c125/c125Thumb.jpg)]({{ site.baseurl }}/c125.html)

*(in collaboration with the Birmingham ElectroAcoustic Sound Theatre, 2026)*  
12 channel widely distributed sound installation across the University of Birmingham Campus, commissioned for the University's 125th anniversary celebrations.

---

## Dendrophone
[![Dendrophone]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/Dendrophone/dendrophoneThumb.jpg)]({{ site.baseurl }}/dendrophone.html)

*(part of the Sensing the Forest project, 2024)*  
16 channel autonomous solar-powered installation, distributed between trees in Alice Holt Forest, Surrey. Sonifies real-time environmental sensor through algorithmic mapping to sound parameters.

---

## Contraption
[![Contraption]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/Contraption/contraptionThumb.png)]({{ site.baseurl }}/contraption.html)

*(a collaboration with Ian Bilson, 2020)*  
Involves five cylindrical columns, each containing 64 speakers. Sounds travel along, around and between these columns such that detailed, playful "mechanical" generation/propagation occurs.

---

## Orbits
[![Orbits]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/Orbits/orbitsThumb.png)]({{ site.baseurl }}/orbits.html)

*(a collaboration with Mike Blow, 2019)*  
Involves six speakers mounted on the ends of tilted spinning arms. Combines sounds derived from Philharmonia instruments with NASA space sounds, considering orbital motions from the atomic to the cosmic.

---

## Cascade
[![Cascade]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/Cascade/cascadeThumb.png)]({{ site.baseurl }}/cascade.html)

*(a collaboration with Ian Bilson, 2018)*  
Presents a flat-panel array of loudspeakers suspended above the listener. It is conceived to operate as a portal to an external (fictional) sonic ‘reality’.  

*Cascade was preceded by the similarly flat-panel* **Studies on Canvas (2004)** *installation.*

---

## Beyond
[![Beyond]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/Beyond/beyondThumb.png)]({{ site.baseurl }}/beyond.html)

*(a collaboration with Ian Bilson, 2014–15)*  
Speakers are placed in the vertices between the triangular panels of geodesic sphere structures. The structures are acoustically transparent, and the installation is conceived such that listeners experience the soundscape beyond the playing loudspeakers as an extension of that presented by the installation itself.  

*Beyond was preceded by the similar, hemispheric* **[DOME]({{ site.baseurl }}/dome.html)** *(2012) installation.*

---

## Cluster
[![Cluster]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/Cluster/clusterThumb.jpg)]({{ site.baseurl }}/cluster.html)

*(2011)*  
A cuboid-shaped suspended walk-through array. The listener is enveloped by the sound, but can navigate it, investigating zones of activity along with individual speakers as point sources.

---

## Studies on Canvas
[![Canvas]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/Canvas/canvasThumb.jpg)]({{ site.baseurl }}/canvas.html)

*(2004)*  
A flat-panel array of loudspeakers behind a blank canvas. The work is designed to be viewed like a conventional painting, with the speaker-space ‘framed’ by the boundaries of the canvas, presenting "pictures" in sound which listeners are encouraged to explore.

---

## Old Joe Sound Sculpture
[![Old Joe Sound Sculpture]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/ojss/ojssThumb.jpg)]({{ site.baseurl }}/ojss.html)

*(2026)*  
4 channel public sound installation complementing the quarter-hourly chimes at the Chamberlain Clock Tower, University of Birmingham.
